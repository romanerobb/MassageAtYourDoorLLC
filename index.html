Tips to get the best benefit from your massage experience

Before your massage therapist arrives:


Tip 1: Adequate space - 10 x 10 allows the therapist to freely move around the table and guide stretching.


Tip 2: Remove all jewelry - allows therapist to have continuous flow to deliver a fluid experience.


Tip 3: Prepare muscles - take a hot shower or bath prior to massage to help relax muscles.


Tip 4: Save time - prepare two sheets, one towel and one pillow case for the massage table.


Tip 5: Ambiance- relaxing music sounds best on your system of choice or the therapist can use her phone.
